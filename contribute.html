Guiding Princples

Stack's visual style is simple yet distinct, making it an ideal starting point for your project whether it be a basic marketing site, or multi-page company presence.

  1. 1

    Flexibility

    Plans will change and things that once worked may have stopped. If its broken, fix it. Let the change happen.

  2. 2

    Gaining Trust

    The Orang Asli have been marginalised for so long, their skepticism of outsiders is understandable. Working with them not on them must be respected always.

  3. 3

    Age does not matter

    Embrace different perspectives. We are a strictly anti-ageist project. Sometimes kids understands kids better. 

  1. 4

    Utilising Platforms

    Check yo' privilege folks. We encourage using your voice, knowledge and privilege platforms to bring awareness and resources to this community.

  2. 5

    Strategise

    There is a solution to almost every problem. Work together, embrace each other's strengths and make the best use of time. Anything is possible if you organise yourselves.

  3. 6

    Remain Kind

    To fellow activists and to everyone you encounter: speak in kind words, do kind things and you're heart will grow, your skin shine.

  1. 7

    Sponge-brain

    Always be ready for absorbing information and learning new skills.

  2. 8

    Evaluate yourself

    Reflect on your thoughts and your actions. Learn from your mistakes

  3. 9

    Have a laugh

    Focus on building broad relationships with people and actually making friends

Now, let's begin
